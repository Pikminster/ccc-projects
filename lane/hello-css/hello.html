Words to know:

  1. Shinigami-Japanese deity of death. Often used as the personification of death, much like the Grim Reaper.
  2. Sub-Term given to a series that was translated via subtitles.
  3. Dub-Term given to a series that was translated via english speaking voice actors, often translations sacrifice parts of the script.
